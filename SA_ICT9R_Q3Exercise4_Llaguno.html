My very first trip to Visayas:


Bohol

Bohol is one of the nieghborhing reigons to Boracay.It is one of Visayas's tourist spots due to it's many tourist attractions. This is possible due to its chocolate hills, Tarsiers and many beaches and resorts.

What to do in Bohol ? Well you can start by enjoying the beaches and beach resorts. Then when you head inland you can go visit the Chocolate mountains,the Tarsiers or more inland resorts and rivers.

I personaly love this place since it gave me many firsts. Like the first time on a plane, first time on a boat, first time under the sea and many more experiences that I wich to repeat.
